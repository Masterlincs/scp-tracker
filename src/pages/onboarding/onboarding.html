Welcome to SCP Tracker

Track what you've read on the SCP Wiki.

Overview

Thanks for installing SCP Tracker. This extension helps you keep track of the SCPs and tales you've read on scp-wiki.wikidot.com.

Quickly see what you've read, apply a comfortable reader mode, and keep your progress available across your browser.

  • Track SCP and tale pages you’ve read
  • See read status at a glance while you browse
  • Use Reader Mode with themes and typography controls
  • Runs only on official SCP Wiki pages

Your data is stored in your browser (locally and with optional sync). No data is sent to external servers.
